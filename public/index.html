ABOUT JJ NOODLES

About:

Thank you for choosing JJ Noodles! We are a San Jose based pop-up shop dedicated to serving the best lobster noodles in the Bay Area. Here at JJ Noodles, we prioritize bringing deliciously fresh food straight to our customers. Specialized in combing restaurant quality ingredients with home cooked flavors! We take pride in only severing platters we would serve at our family parties.
